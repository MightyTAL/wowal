Research Proposal

How does Data-Driven Innovation affect the environmental performance of organizations?

In order to address the shortcomings of previous research, this research proposal adopts the perspective of dynamic capability theory and proposes a theoretical model. The aim is to explore the impact of DDI on environmental performance in enterprises as well as reveal the mediating role of green innovation and the moderating role of environmental responsibility.

Research on the impact of innovation on informal employment

Given the ongoing wave of new technological revolution characterized by artificial intelligence, quantum information, and mobile communication, innovation has emerged as a significant force driving social production mode transformation. Consequently, it has multiple implications for employment. The impact of innovation on informal employment remains uncertain and warrants further investigation. It is essential to clarify how these changes affect workers at the lower end of the labor market.
